About

Bebemos (“bi-beer-mos”) is Newtown’s relaxation headquarters.

Enjoy any of our 60+ international and local boutique bottled beers, our revolving selection of 11 taps, ever popular Latin-influenced menu and fine wines and cocktails.

Either park up next to the fire inside, or protected from the elements in the Cabana - the heated beer garden out back.
